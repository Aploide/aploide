Privacy Policy

Our "Eyed" apps does not store any data about users.

Our browser "Eyed Out" does not store any data about users.

And we try to get the browser as private as possible, limiting also the websites that people uses to track you.

We created the browser based off WebKit so you can check more information about it on https://webkit.org/blog/category/privacy/

Third-Party APIs

Even tho our browser uses Third-Party APIs we in Aploide keep the user information hidden during the API call.

If you have any questions about the App or the APIs, feel free to contact me at yo@aploi.de
